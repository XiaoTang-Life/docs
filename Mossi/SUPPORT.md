## Getting Started

### 1. Download and Install
1. Download **Mossi** from the App Store
2. Open the app and grant necessary permissions
3. Customize your pet's name and personality

### 2. Add Widget to Home Screen
1. Long press on your home screen
2. Tap the `+` button in the top-left corner
3. Search for "Mossi"
4. Choose **Small** or **Medium** widget size
5. Tap "Add Widget"
6. Position the widget on your home screen

### 3. Customize Your Pet
1. Open Mossi app
2. Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation
3. Set up:
   - **Pet Name**: Give your companion a unique name (max 10 characters)
   - **Your Name**: How your pet calls you (max 10 characters)
   - **Personality**: Choose from 5 unique personalities
   - **Language**: Select your preferred language

### Widget Questions

#### Q: How do I add the widget to my home screen?
**A**: 
1. Long press on your home screen until icons wiggle
2. Tap the `+` button in the top-left corner

**A**: Try these steps:

**A**:

## Troubleshooting

### Widget Not Showing
**Solutions**:
1. Open the main Mossi app at least once
2. Grant necessary permissions if prompted
3. Ensure iOS is updated to 16.0+
4. Remove widget and add it again
5. Restart your iPhone

### Widget Not Updating
**Solutions**:
1. Open the Mossi app to trigger a refresh
2. Change mood in the app manually
3. Check if Low Power Mode is enabled
4. Wait a few minutes for system to update
5. Remove and re-add the widget

### App Crashes on Launch
**Solutions**:
1. Restart your iPhone
2. Ensure iOS is updated to 16.0+
3. Delete and reinstall the app
4. Contact support with crash details
